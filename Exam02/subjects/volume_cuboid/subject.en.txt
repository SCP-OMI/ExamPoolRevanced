Assignment name  : volume_cuboid
Expected files   : volume_cuboid.c
Allowed functions:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Write a function to find volume of cuboid
    - mathematical formula of cuboid volume (l * b * h).
    with  'l' length of cuboid, 'b' base of cuboid, and h height of cuboid;

Your function must be declared as follows:
    int     volume_cuboid(int l, int b, int h):

Example : 
    $> volume_cuboid(2, 3, 4) = 24  
    $> volume_cuboid(5, 5, 5) = 125
